Your Fundamental Right to Full, Fast, & Free Access to the Law

  • From the US Constitution to your home city's code, American laws are the people's public property.
    • Nothing should stand between you and accessing the law.
    • Nothing should prevent you from understanding it in your own terms
    • Nothing should restrict your basic right to use and share the law itself, when you want and how you want.
  • Free and equal citizens, in a free and equal country, need free and equal access to the law. “America Decoded” will not stop growing - and our entire OpenGov team will not stop working - until everyone enjoys full, fast and free access to our nation's laws.

Access Denied: Removing Barriers Between You and Your Laws

  • But there are real barriers blocking access to the law at all levels of government, from federal clean water rules to local leash laws.
    • Insane copyright restrictions on the law could land you in court or worse… just for posting a piece of public law on the Internet.
    • Even when the law is online, worthless websites and disorganized data - like paper-based PDFs - make it all but invisible to everyday people.
    • Complex, confusing legal jargon only makes securing your rights as a citizen that much harder.
  • “America Decoded” exists to demolish these undemocratic obstacles with a potent mix of computing power and people power.
